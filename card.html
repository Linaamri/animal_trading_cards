Domestic Cat

Meowing is not the language of cats; it was developed by cats to communicate with people

  • Family: Felidae
  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Skills: communication, hunting, and flexibility

Cats are adorable and clean creatures that spend most of their time grooming themselves and sleeping as they averagely spend 16 hours a day sleeping. In addition, there are about 64 different cat breeds that differ based on their distinctive appearance
